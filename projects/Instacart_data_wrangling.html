Instacart Data Wrangling

Data preprocessing and exploratory analysis of Instacart user orders to identify patterns and trends.

Project Overview

Problem: Understanding customer purchase behavior and product selection trends.

Solution: Data preprocessing including type correction, handling missing values, and removing duplicates.

Technologies & Tools: Python (pandas), Jupyter Notebook, VS Code

Exploratory Data Analysis

  • Orders per hour of the day
  • Orders by day of the week
  • Time until next order
  • Difference between Wednesday and Saturday for 'order_hour_of_day'
  • Distribution of items per order

Conclusion

  • Identified peak ordering times (9 a.m.) and most active shopping days (Sunday & Monday).
  • Banana is the most frequently ordered and repeated product.
  • Average product reorder rate is above 50%, useful for segmentation and promotions.
  • Data preprocessing ensured high-quality data for subsequent analysis.

Project: Instacart Data Wrangling

Instacart is a grocery delivery platform where customers can place an order and have it delivered.

The Problem

The goal is to understand patterns in user shopping behavior and trends in product selection.

Solution

Data preprocessing steps included:

  • Checking and correcting data types.
  • Identifying and handling missing values.
  • Detecting and removing duplicates.

Technologies & Tools

  • Python (pandas)
  • Jupyter Notebook
  • VS Code

Exploratory Data Analysis

  • Orders per hour of the day
  • Orders by day of the week
  • Time until next order
  • Difference between Wednesday and Saturday for 'order_hour_of_day'
  • Distribution of items per order

Conclusion

  • Peak order time is at 9 a.m., and Sunday/Monday are the busiest shopping days.
  • On average, users wait 30 days before placing another order.
  • Bananas are the most ordered and frequently repeated product.
  • Repeat purchase rate is above 50%, which helps segment customers and prioritize product promotions.
  • Data preprocessing ensured high-quality data for further analysis.

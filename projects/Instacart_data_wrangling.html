Instacart — Data Wrangling & Exploratory Data Analysis

Data wrangling, cleaning, and visualization to understand customer ordering patterns.

Project Overview

This project analyzes Instacart customer orders to identify patterns in purchasing behavior, frequency of orders, time-based patterns, and product distribution. The analysis includes data cleaning, feature engineering, aggregation, and exploratory data visualization.

Exploratory Data Analysis

  • Orders per hour of the day
  • Orders by day of the week
  • Time until next order
  • Difference between Wednesday and Saturday
  • Distribution of items per order

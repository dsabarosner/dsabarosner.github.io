Instacart Data Wrangling

Description

Instacart is a grocery delivery platform where customers can place orders and have them delivered. The project aims to identify patterns in user purchase behavior and trends in selected products.

Problem

The goal is to understand patterns in user purchasing behavior and trends in product selection.

Solution / Data Preprocessing

  • Check and correct data types
  • Identify and handle missing values
  • Identify and remove duplicate entries

Technologies & Tools

  • Python (pandas)
  • Jupyter Notebook
  • VS Code

General Conclusions

  • Identified peak purchase hours at 9 a.m., and peak days being Sunday and Monday. Average waiting time for the next order is 30 days.
  • Bananas are the most frequently ordered and repeated product, often the first selected in carts.
  • Average product re-order rate is above 50%, useful for customer and product segmentation in promotions.
  • Data preprocessing was essential to ensure data quality and enable further analysis.

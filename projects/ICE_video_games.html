Video Game Market Analysis

Exploratory and statistical analysis of real-world video game sales data to support commercial and advertising strategies.

Project Overview

This integrative project aims to identify market patterns, platform dynamics, and success factors in video game performance.

Technologies & Tools: Python (pandas, numpy, scipy, matplotlib, seaborn, math), Jupyter Notebook, VS Code

Tasks Completed

  • Data preparation, cleaning, and conversion of data types.
  • Handling missing values (imputing or removing as needed).
  • Feature engineering—created total sales variable.
  • Deep EDA including platform lifecycles, genre performance, critic & user score correlations.
  • Regional breakdown of game performance.
  • Hypothesis testing on platform and genre user ratings.

Exploratory Data Analysis

  • Games released per year
  • Sales by platform
  • Video game sales per year
  • Distribution of global sales by platform
  • Genre sales distribution

Conclusion

  • The video game market has shown strong volatility and dynamism.
  • Outlier games significantly influence platform sales totals.
  • Platform maturity varies widely, requiring platform-specific commercial strategies.
  • Regional trends differ remarkably between Japanese and Western markets.
  • ESRB ratings have limited impact globally but matter for all-ages markets.
